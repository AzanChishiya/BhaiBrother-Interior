About Us

◆

Est. in Karachi, Pakistan

A Legacy of Luxury Living

Al-Abdaan Interior was born from a deep passion for beautiful spaces. Founded by Abdaan Arsalan, the shop started as a humble dream to bring world class interior quality to the homes of Karachi at honest prices.

Nestled at Erum Shopping Emporium, Buffer Zone, we have grown into one of Karachi's most trusted names in curtains, custom drapes, luxury sofas, carved furniture and false ceiling solutions.

Every piece in our collection is hand-selected. We believe your home deserves nothing less than the finest — and we make that standard accessible to every family.
