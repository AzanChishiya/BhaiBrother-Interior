Our Story

About BhaiBrother Interior

◆

Est. in Karachi, Pakistan

A Legacy of Luxury Living

BhaiBrother Interior was born from a deep passion for beautiful spaces. Founded by Arsalan Nawaz, the shop started as a humble dream — to bring world-class interior quality to the homes of Karachi at honest prices.

Nestled in the heart of the city at Erum Shopping Emporium, Buffer Zone, we have grown into one of Karachi's most trusted names in curtains, custom drapes, luxury sofas, carved furniture, and false ceiling solutions.

Every piece in our collection is hand-selected. We believe that your home deserves nothing less than the finest — and we make that standard accessible to every family.

AN

Arsalan Nawaz

Founder & Owner · BhaiBrother Interior
